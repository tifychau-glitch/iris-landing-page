FAQ

Questions

Do I need to know how to code?▶
No. IRIS installs with a single action into Claude Desktop. No terminal. No Python. No setup scripts. You download the ZIP, drop it in, and it runs.
How is this different from just using ChatGPT?▶
ChatGPT Iris
Forgets you the moment the session ends. Every session builds on the last.
No personality. Infinitely adjustable. One personality. Non-negotiable.
Answers when you ask. Shows up whether you ask or not.
ChatGPT is a conversation. Iris is a system with a job.
What else can she do?▶
Iris runs as a full operating system inside Claude Desktop. Accountability is her personality. Productivity is her job. She manages tasks, tracks commitments, and keeps your day moving while making sure you actually follow through on what you said you would. Think of her less as an app and more as someone who lives inside your computer and has opinions about how you spend your time.
Do I need Claude Desktop already?▶
Yes. Iris runs inside Claude Desktop, which is a free download from Anthropic. You will need the Pro plan ($20/month) for full access. If you don't have it yet, you'll set it up as part of the install process. It's straightforward. See the cost breakdown above for the full picture.
What device does this run on?▶
Iris runs on Mac and PC, anywhere Claude Desktop is supported. If you can run Claude Desktop, you can run Iris.
How is this different from a to-do app?▶
A to-do app doesn't care if you finish. Iris does. She tracks your completion rate, notices your patterns, and adjusts her tone based on your behavior. A to-do app is passive. Iris is not.
Can I control how harsh she gets?▶
Yes. During setup you calibrate her max level (1-5). She will never exceed it. If you set max to 3, she stays there even when your completion rate tanks.
Is this a subscription?▶
No. One-time payment. IRIS is yours. Future updates are included for all paying tiers.
What do I get immediately after purchase?▶
You get a download link instantly. Inside the ZIP is Iris's install file and a step-by-step installation guide. A confirmation email with your download link is sent to your inbox. From download to first conversation is about six steps.
Can she track more than one goal?▶
Yes. During onboarding Iris gets to know you. Your business, your personal goals, what you are working toward and why. You give her a few goals. She remembers all of them. She will reference them across check-ins, notice patterns, and connect the dots between what you said you wanted and what you are actually doing.
What if I want to add more skills later?▶
Iris is built on a modular skill system. Accountability is what she ships with, but she is designed to grow with you. If you later decide you need her to help with content, email, task management, or anything else, you just add a new skill on top of what is already there. Nothing breaks. Nothing resets. She keeps everything she already knows about you and picks up the new capability like she always had it.
Is my data private?▶
Yes. Iris runs locally inside Claude Desktop on your own machine. Your conversations, your goals, and your check-in history are not stored on external servers or shared with anyone. What you tell Iris stays between you and your computer.
